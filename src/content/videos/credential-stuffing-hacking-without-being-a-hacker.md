Credential stuffing is a major threat that impacts everyone, and I’ve made a quick 6-minute video to explain why this attack technique is so critical to understand.

Here’s the issue: attackers don’t need advanced skills to hack you. By using leaked usernames and passwords from past breaches, they can log in as you without breaking a sweat. It’s not about being targeted specifically, it’s about being in the blast radius.

In the video, I discuss:

How credential stuffing works.
Why so many people and businesses fall victim to it.
Simple yet powerful defenses: using a password manager and enabling multi-factor authentication.
This is a problem that affects all of us, whether it’s your personal accounts, your parents, your children, or even your business. The key to staying secure is taking action now.
