I am speaking at **DefCamp 2022** in Bucharest on November 10 and 11, Central and Eastern Europe's largest annual hacking and information security conference, drawing more than 1,600 people from over 30 countries.

Business Review previewed the lineup and the backdrop for this year's event, leading with a striking statistic: ransomware attacks in just the first three months of 2022 more than doubled the total for all of the previous year. You can read it here: [DefCamp 2022 coverage on Business Review](https://business-review.eu/bucharest-going-out/events/the-number-of-ransomware-attacks-in-just-three-months-of-2022-has-more-than-doubled-compared-to-the-total-number-of-attacks-in-the-last-year-238760).

My talk is on planning and building an offensive SOC: turning alerts into opportunities by pairing continuous offensive testing with detection, so defenders learn from a real attacker's perspective instead of waiting to be surprised. The recording is linked from my [talks & media](/talks/) page.

If you are at DefCamp, come say hi.
