Learn penetration testing: www.sans.org/sec560
Presented by: Chris Dale

Before Chris Dale started pen testing full-time, he sat as a CISO having to procure third party pen tests. More often than not, the pen tests were of poor quality, providing less than ideal value to the client. Now, several years later, and with several years managing the Pen Testing Team at Netsecurity, a lot of great ideas and ways to provide higher value has been condensed into this talk. This talk will help both customers and pen testers get higher value from their pen tests.

Chris Dale is a SANS Instructor and teaches our most popular training course, SANS SEC504: Hacker Tools, Techniques, Exploits, and Incident Handling all throughout the EMEA region. www.sans.org/sec504
