Penetration Testing w/Chris Dale

When our data disappears, when intrusions occur, or when major companies face a data breach, who do you call? Public services are out of capacity!

Chris Dale is a SANS instructor and serves as the Chief Operating Officer (COO) and Co-Founder at Riverside Security. He's one of the digital cape-wearing superheroes who come to the rescue of large businesses in need.

Many people today experience being hacked, and recently, Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks have been more relevant than ever. The police, the Parliament, municipalities, and major private companies face numerous data breaches every day. But even with all this happening, do you need to give up?

Chris provides clear technological and security advice. To become a SANS instructor, you need to pass a comprehensive entrance test, and Chris was one of the top performers in his class. EYD is happy to receive good advice from this man and to share it with our listeners!
