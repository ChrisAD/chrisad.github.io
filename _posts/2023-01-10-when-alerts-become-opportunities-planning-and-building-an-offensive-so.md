A DefCamp 2022 presentation by Chris Dale, Principal Instructor at SANS Institute.

The most important annual hacking and cybersecurity conference in Central and Eastern Europe returned onsite in Bucharest, on 10th-11th November 2022, to bring together the infosec expert community in the region.

Since 2011 to date, DefCamp has managed to attract over 11,000 participants from over 55 countries and 150 cities, passionate about hacking and vulnerabilities, and interested in developing their technical skills. 

This time, DefCamp brought together more than 1,600 participants, from entrepreneurs to software developers, and especially cybersecurity enthusiasts and specialists from the public sector and academia from over 30 countries.
